Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

IB CS OOP Terminal ("the app") is a free study tool for IB Computer Science HL students. This policy explains what data is collected and how it is used.

What we collect

  • Google account info — when you sign in with Google, we receive your name and email address. This is used only to identify your account.
  • Study progress — your quiz scores, questions seen, and wrong-answer queue are stored in a database to sync across your devices.
  • API keys — if you optionally enter a third-party AI provider key (Groq, Gemini, etc.), it is stored in your account's database row, which is protected by Row Level Security. Only you can access it.

What we don't collect

  • We do not collect payment information.
  • We do not sell or share your data with third parties.
  • We do not use tracking pixels, ads, or analytics services.

How data is stored

Data is stored using Supabase, a hosted database service. Each user's data is isolated using Row Level Security — no user can access another user's data.

Third-party AI providers

If you use the AI grading feature, your answer text is sent directly from your browser to the AI provider you configured (Groq, Gemini, OpenRouter, etc.). This is governed by their respective privacy policies. The app does not proxy or store these requests.

Deleting your data

You can clear your progress and API key at any time from the Settings section of the app. To request full account deletion, contact us at the link below.
